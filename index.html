UNIVERSITY OF CHITTAGONG

Founded on 3 December,1965 | By Fazlul Qadir Chaudhry

LOCATION

The University of Chittagong is a public research university in Fatehpur Union of Hathazari Upazila,
22 km north of Chittagong.Its 1,754 acres campus is the largest among Bangladeshi universities.

Facilities

Library

Chittagong University's collection of about 350,000 books,
and over 40,000 bound journals is housed in a 56,700 square feet library built in 1990.

Museum

Chittagong University Museum was established in 1973 with artifacts from the
History Department including fossils found amongst the Nasirabad hills.
In 2016, The Daily Star donated a Chittagong history archive to the museum.

Shuttle train

The university shuttle train is operated by Bangladesh Railway eastern division and
monitored by university authority.Trains leave Chattogram railway station and
Sholoshohor railway station, and city-bound trains leave Chattogram University railway station.
